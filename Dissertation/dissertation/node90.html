Simulated Annealing on the FAP

The <#2812#>SA<#2812#> algorithm, as with the <#2813#>TS<#2813#> algorithm, has achieved good results in other optimisation problems, as mentioned in section #sec:SAIntroduction#2814>. Due to its success on other NP-Complete optimisation problems, the <#2815#>SA<#2815#> algorithm has also been applied to the <#2816#>FAP<#2816#>.

In literature by Carlo Mannino and Gianpaolo Oriolo[#SolvingSuperIntervalGraphs##1###] the <#2818#>SA<#2818#> algorithm is applied to the <#2819#>FAP<#2819#>. The resulting <#2820#>SA<#2820#> algorithm was benchmarked on the <#2821#>COST<#2821#> 259 Siemens benchmark instances. The results obtained by the authors are presented in table~#tab:SA#2822>.
