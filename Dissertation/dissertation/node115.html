Pheromone Evaporation

Initially when the pheromone concept was first implemented the ants of the colony rapidly converged on a solution[#CompuIntelligenceIntro##1###]. The search space was not adequately explored and the produced solution was of local optima[#AntsAndStigmergy##1###]. To combat this premature convergence and force the ants to explore the search space more, the concept of pheromone evaporation was introduced[#AntIntroTrends##1###,#AntSurvey##1###].

Real pheromones used by ants to mark a particular link to a node is not permanent and over time the strength of the pheromone deteriorates until it eventually disappears[#CompuIntelligenceIntro##1###]. Pheromone deteriorating is known in the literature as pheromone evaporation[#CompuIntelligenceIntro##1###]. The pheromone will not completely evaporate as long there are ants traversing the defined link reinforcing the pheromone. Equation #eq:pheromoneevapuration#3907> was first introduced in the <#3908#>AS<#3908#> [#CompuIntelligenceIntro##1###,#AntSurvey##1###]. Most subsequent algorithms that are a form of the <#3910#>ACO<#3910#> class of algorithms also use the concept of pheromone evaporation, but they either use the standard equation or develop their own variant [#CompuIntelligenceIntro##1###,#AntsAndStigmergy##1###].

A more aggressive form of pheromone evaporation is added to the <#3912#>AS<#3912#> discussed in the research done by Gambardella et. al[#AntQAP##1###]. The more aggressive form works beside the already present pheromone evaporation, but this form seeks to add an additional search phase called diversification[#AntQAP##1###]. The aim of the diversification phase is to lead the algorithm into another direction of the search space[#AntQAP##1###]. This is done in an attempt to avoid local minima and stagnation[#AntQAP##1###].

In the ant system developed by Gambardella et. al. the algorithm continually monitors the current best solution and keeps a history of recent best solutions[#AntQAP##1###]. If the algorithm starts to notice that solutions are cycling or that the current best solution has not changed for a certain number of iterations, the algorithm activates the diversification phase[#AntQAP##1###]. In this phase the algorithm is forced to re-search the search space to create new solutions, as it cannot rely on previous historical information provided by the pheromone trails[#AntQAP##1###].
