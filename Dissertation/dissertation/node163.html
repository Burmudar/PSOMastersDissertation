The Performance of the PSO

In this section the effects of the changes made to the FAP PSO algorithm in terms of the results is discussed.

Firstly, the effect of the two developed velocity methods in terms of the results is described. In section~#sec:diffglobalschemes#6638> the three global schemes that are used by the algorithm are discussed. Finally this section concludes with the effect of a larger population size on solution quality rather than a small population.
